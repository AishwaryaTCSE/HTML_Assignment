About Lord Rama

Introduction to Lord Rama

Lord Rama is one of the most revered deities in Hinduism, known as the seventh incarnation of the god Vishnu. He is considered the ideal man, known for his virtues, ethics, and his role in the epic Ramayana.

The Life of Lord Rama

Lord Rama was born to King Dasharatha and Queen Kausalya in the city of Ayodhya.

Lord Rama's Exile and Return

Lord Rama was exiled to the forest for fourteen years due to a promise made by his father. During his exile, he defeated many demons, including the mighty Ravana, who abducted his wife Sita.
His return to Ayodhya after victory marks the celebration of Diwali, the festival of lights, where people honor the triumph of good over evil.
